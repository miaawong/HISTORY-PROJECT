    • Facts about Mercury

    • In astronomy mythology, Mercury was the Roman version of the Greek god Hermes. He was the messenger for the other gods.
    • Mercury is small and does not have enough gravity to hold onto an atmposhere. Because Mercury is so close to the Sun, there is no air on Mercury. The Sun's solar winds blow away any atmosphere.
    • The surface of Mercury can reach about 800 degrees. During night time, it can plummet down to -300 degrees.
