    • Facts about Uranus

    • Classified as ice giant, composed of hydrogen and helium. Contains water, ammonia, and methane.

    • The coldest planetary atmosphere in the Solar System, the interior is mainly composed of ices and rocks

    • Discovered by William Herschel in 1781

    • Only planet name that is derived from a figure in Greek mythology, the Greek god of the sky Ouranos.
