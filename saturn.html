    • Facts about Saturn

    • With his scope magnifying up to 30 times, Galileo sees Saturn as a three-body system. Shown above is Galileo's sketch from 1616.

    • Saturn is named after the Roman god of agriculture.

    • Saturn has a ring system that consists of nine main rings, composed mostly of ice particles with siome rocky debris and dust.

    • 62 moons are known to orbit Saturn. Titan is Saturn's largest moon, bigger than the planet Mercury.
