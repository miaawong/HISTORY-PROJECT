  • Development of Technology

    • Facts about Jupiter

    • Galileo Galilei was the first to discover that the Moon was cratered, that the Sun was marked with sunspots, and that Jupiter had four satellites in orbit around it (named the Galilean Moons)
    • Based only on descriptions of the first telescope created in the Netherlands, Galileo made a telescope with about 3x magnification. He later made improved versions with up to about 30x magnification.
    • Jupiter is a gas giant, it is primarily composed of hydrogen, with a quarter of its mass being helium
    • Romans named Jupiter after their god.
